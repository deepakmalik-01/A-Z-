About the Owner

Firasat Khan

Meet the man behind A TO Z Car Service — a dedicated automotive expert with over 20 years of hands-on experience in the field. Starting his career as a mechanic, he spent 3 years working in Bangalore, gaining exposure to modern vehicle technologies and service standards. He then returned to Moradabad, where he honed his skills further for 17 years, earning the trust and respect of countless vehicle owners.

10 years ago, driven by his passion for cars and commitment to quality service, he founded A TO Z Car Service. Since then, he has been running the garage full-time, delivering reliable, efficient, and honest service to the local community.

His deep understanding of vehicle systems, attention to detail, and customer-first approach are the backbone of our garage. Whether it's routine maintenance or complex repairs, you can count on his expertise to get your car back on the road—running like new.
